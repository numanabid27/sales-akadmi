OBJECTIVE

Objectives Of The Measure

After completing the course, our participants are able to program apps for Android and iOS devices (smartphones, tablets, watches).

The course concept is based on practical application. The participants develop a project portfolio consisting of a click prototype, a console program and 2 own apps (Android, iOS).

At the end of the course, the participants receive their carrier certificate for successfully completing the course and then have the opportunity to obtain the recognized qualification “Mobile Developer (IHK)”.
